Chocolate Cake

Chocolate Cake Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 2 cup white sugar
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Mix in the eggs, vanilla, and oil on medium speed. Stir in the boiling water
  3. Pour the batter into prepared pans. Bake until a toothpick comes out clean

Directions

  • Step1: Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans
  • Step2: Stir sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a large bowl.
  • Step3: Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla; mix for 2 minutes on medium speed with an electric mixer.
  • Step4: Stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin.
  • Step5: Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
  • Step6: Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 30 to 35 minutes.
    Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  • Step7: Enjoy!

Glossary

1. Cocoa Powder
- A dry, bitter powder made from roasted cocoa beans; provides the main chocolate flavor in the cake.
2. Creaming
- The process of beating butter and sugar together until light and fluffy, often used as the first step in cake batter preparation.
3. Fold
- To gently mix dry and wet ingredients (like egg whites into batter) to maintain air and volume.
4. Sift
- To pass dry ingredients (like flour or cocoa) through a sieve to remove lumps and aerate the mixture.
5. Ganache
- A rich chocolate glaze or filling made by mixing chocolate and warm cream.
6. Preheat
- To heat the oven to a specific temperature before placing the cake inside to ensure even baking.
7. Toothpick Test
- A method to check if the cake is done by inserting a toothpick into the center—if it comes out clean, the cake is ready.
